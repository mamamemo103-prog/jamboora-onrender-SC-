FAQ

Short answers about Jamboora, virtual coins, and how play works.

Is Jamboora free?

Yes. You can explore the lobby and play without paying real money on this site. Sessions use virtual coins only.

What are virtual coins?

In-app points for pacing and progression. They are not cash, cannot be withdrawn, and hold no monetary value.

Can I win real money?

No. Jamboora is entertainment-only. There are no real-money prizes or payouts.

Which games are available?

Slots, roulette, blackjack, poker-style tables, and wheel-led titles — listed in the lobby.

Who can I contact?

Email hello@jamboora.com for product questions.
